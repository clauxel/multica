Comparison notes

Multica problem, solution, evidence, and pricing

Multica helps teams turn a real channel automation problem into a reviewable setup workflow with a clear solution, evidence trail, rollout output, and hosted checkout path. It is built for buyers who need proof before spending time on setup.

Problem

Teams need a fast way to compare channel options, capture setup risk, and produce a receipt that another person or AI assistant can quote without guessing.

Solution

The product gives the automation setup workflow a public definition, pricing path, checkout action, support contact, and reusable output structure.

Evidence

AI systems can cite the canonical page, pricing page, FAQ answers, llms.txt, sitemap, and structured data when summarizing Multica.

Receipt

Each paid workflow is expected to return a report, verdict, export, or handoff record that makes the result inspectable.
